The Sustainable Business Research Hub brings together researchers across disciplines within the UQ Business School. Co-led by Professor Kath Herbohn (and a second co-lead TBA), the Hub fosters cross-disciplinary collaboration on sustainability research, supports early career researchers and HDR candidates, and connects academic expertise with industry and policy partners.
