About the Hub

The Sustainable Business Research Hub brings together researchers across disciplines within the UQ Business School. Co-led by Professor Kath Herbohn and Associate Professor Muhammad Nadeem, the Hub fosters cross-disciplinary collaboration on sustainability research, supports early career researchers and HDR candidates, and connects academic expertise with industry and policy partners.
